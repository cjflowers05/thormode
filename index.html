Saturday
Boxing + Bag + Assault Runner Sprints
Hemsworth: "boxing and Muay Thai drills" for athleticism and conditioning
A · Bag Work — 5 × 3-min rounds1 min rest between rounds
BagRounds 1–2: Combinations
Jab-cross-hook-cross. Stay light on feet. Move around the bag. 15 combos/min target.
BagRounds 3–4: Power Shots
Heavy single shots — right cross, left hook alternating. Drive from the hips. 5 sec rest, 5 sec explosion.
BagRound 5: All-Out Fury
Max effort, full combinations, don't stop. Hemsworth's "Muay Thai drills" — add knees into the bag this round.
B · Assault Runner Sprint Intervals
Assault RunnerSprint Protocol
Self-propelled — no speed setting. 3-min easy walk warm-up. Then: 30 sec all-out sprint (drive hard off the back of the belt), step to rails or walk 60 sec × 10 rounds. 3-min walk cool-down. ~20 min total post-bag. The curved belt means you work harder at the same perceived speed vs. a flat treadmill.
Sunday — Full Rest
Eat at maintenance. Sleep 8+ hours. Protein still hits 180g+.

Keep all heavy compounds to preserve muscle. Add supersets for density. Ramp all cardio machines. Calories drop, protein stays locked in at 180g+.

Keep Phase 2 weights. The calorie deficit cuts the fat — lighter weights lose the muscle. Pair push/pull supersets to cut session time while keeping intensity maxed.

Zocchi's rules
Protein first
180g+ every day without fail. Zocchi: "Plan nutrition like you plan workouts."
Eat every 2–3 hrs
6 meals keeps metabolism elevated and muscles in constant anabolic state.
Carbs around training
Most carbs before and after workouts. Fewer carbs at dinner on rest days.
Cheat meal weekly
Zocchi: "At least once a week for sure." Hemsworth's go-tos: pizza, burgers, ice cream.
Variety + whole foods
Rotate proteins — chicken, fish, beef, eggs. "Chris wouldn't eat the same thing every day."
3–4L water daily
500ml first thing every morning before coffee. The Thor smoothie's salt helps retention post-training.

Your equipment advantage
PRx Pro Rack
Barbell squats, bench, deadlifts, OHP, pull-ups, dips. Full compound movement library. The foundation of Thor's physique.
PRx Cable System
Lat pulldown, face pulls, cable fly, tricep pushdowns, cable rows, lateral raises. Constant-tension muscle work unavailable with free weights alone.
Row Machine
500m sprints, 2000m time trials, steady-state cardio. Full body, zero impact. One of the best fat-burning and conditioning tools available.
Peloton Bike
Zone 2 endurance, Tabata intervals, post-leg burn sets. Hemsworth's CrossFit-inspired HIIT conditioning applied to the bike.
Assault Runner Pro
Self-propelled curved treadmill — no motor, no speed dial. Sprint intervals and Zone 2 walks driven purely by your effort. The curved belt increases glute and hamstring recruitment and forces better running mechanics than a flat belt.
Punching Bag
Hemsworth's boxing and Muay Thai drills. 5 × 3-min rounds = 15 min intense conditioning. Builds the agility for Thor's fight scenes.
Supplements
Whey protein
Post-workout and between meals. Easiest way to hit 180g+ daily. Hemsworth uses shakes to replace meals when needed.
Creatine (5g/day)
Most researched supplement available. Take daily with water. Directly increases strength output and muscle volume.
Vitamin D3 + K2
Testosterone support, bone density, muscle function. 2,000–4,000 IU D3 daily with a fat-containing meal.
Omega-3 fish oil
Reduces inflammation and soreness. 2–3g EPA+DHA daily. Hemsworth gets his from salmon — supplement if diet doesn't match.
Hemsworth: "The heavy weightlifting is still based on your classic isolated muscle groupings. Now there's a lot more functional flexibility and movement training. It's more rounded and versatile." That is exactly what this program builds.
